# Useful Prompts

## Learning

- "I want to learn about [insert topic]. Tell me the most critical 20% that helps me understand 80%".
- "I'm a beginner and want to get better at [skill]. Suggest 5 hands-on project ideas to practice it".
- "Give me the best learning resources (books, videos, podcasts, courses) for [topic]. Make sure they fit different learning styles, especially visual learners"
    - "Now shortlist only the free ones"
    - "Now pick the ones that have practical exercises".
- "Explain [concept] like I'm 5".

## Act as a...

- "Act as a world-class copywriter with 10+ years of experience. Help me write a blog post about [topic]."
- "Pretend to be an SEO expert. Audit my website and suggest fixes to improve rankings".
- "Act as a Sales Manager with 15 years of experience. Rewrite my pitch to make it more persuasive".

## Interactive

### Ask for clarity

- "Ask me clarifying questions to help you create..."
- "What information do you need from me to...?"
- "Before answering, ask me any clarifying questions you need to complete this task perfectly."
- "Please interview me about this topic to gather the necessary details before drafting the response."
- "Read my request below. Do not provide the solution until I have confirmed I have answered all your questions."
- "Act as a senior consultant. Your goal is to diagnose my problem. Start by asking me a series of questions to understand my current situation."
- "Act as a UX Researcher. I have a product idea. Ask me questions to help define the user persona."

### Iterative

- "Let's work on this step-by-step. Stop after every step to ask for my feedback before moving to the next."

### Example

> "I need help writing a cover letter for a job application.
>
> Constraint: Do not write the letter yet. Action: Ask me 5 specific questions about my experience and the job description so you can tailor the letter effectively. Once I answer, then you can write the draft."
